Q2:-How to embed audio and video in a webpage?

ans:-

Audio:-

audio tag embed <audio>tag. To use an audio tag, its Controls attribute is added. Like autoplay,Pause,Volume.
You will use Source element. You can do this by providing a link to your audio inside the src="" attribute inside the <sources> element.
And you will also give the type of the audio like audio/ogg,mp3.

Video:-

video tag embed <video>tag. To use an video tag, its Controls attribute is added. Like autoplay,Pause,Volume. You can also set hight,width You will use Source element.
You can do this by providing a link to your video inside the src="" attribute inside the <sources> element.
And you will also give the type of the video like video/ogg,mp3.
